About Miguel Gastin

The story behind the brand

Our Journey

Miguel Gastin started this brand with a simple vision: to create clothing that transcends fashion and becomes a medium for storytelling. Born from a passion for cinema, philosophy, and hip-hop culture, Miguel began designing tees that merged these worlds into wearable narratives. What started as a personal project in 2020 has evolved into a movement where each piece represents a carefully crafted story. Today, our cinematic tees are worn by collectors worldwide who understand that clothing is more than just fabric—it's a statement of identity and a connection to something greater than ourselves.

Our Mission & Vision

We believe in the power of purposeful design. Our mission is to create cinematic tees that inspire, provoke thought, and connect people through shared narratives. We're committed to maintaining the highest quality standards while keeping our collections limited and exclusive, ensuring that every piece remains meaningful and valuable. By blending the discipline and honor of samurai philosophy with the raw authenticity of hip-hop culture, we create designs that challenge conventions and celebrate individuality. Our vision is to build a global community of collectors who understand that true style is about substance, story, and the courage to wear your truth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our cinematic tees and collections.

Our cinematic tees are designed with visual storytelling at their core. Each design is inspired by film aesthetics—bold compositions, striking imagery, and compelling typography that captures the essence of cinema. We draw from the narrative language of movies to create tees that function as wearable frames from untold stories.

Every design goes through a rigorous creative process where we consider color psychology, visual hierarchy, and the emotional impact of the final piece. The result is clothing that doesn't just look good—it tells a story and makes a statement about the wearer's values and aesthetic sensibilities.

Our chaptered collections are organized like chapters in a book. Each chapter represents a distinct narrative arc with its own set of designs, themes, and visual language. Chapter One might explore samurai philosophy, while Chapter Two delves into hip-hop culture, and Chapter Three brings them together.

Collectors can purchase individual tees or complete entire chapters. Each chapter is limited to a specific number of pieces, and once a chapter closes, we move on to the next. This creates a natural collecting experience where enthusiasts can build a cohesive narrative collection over time.

We typically release new chapters quarterly, giving collectors time to complete previous chapters while building anticipation for what's coming next.

We stand behind the quality of our products with a 100% Satisfaction Guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase for any reason, we offer a full refund within 30 days of purchase.

Simply reach out to us at contact@miguelgastin.com with your order number and reason for return. We'll provide you with a prepaid shipping label, and once we receive and inspect the item, we'll process your refund promptly.

We only ask that items be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached. This ensures we can resell limited edition pieces to other collectors who will appreciate them.

We offer fast worldwide shipping with an estimated delivery time of 5 business days. This includes processing time and transit time.

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days, and then we ship via a reliable international carrier. You'll receive a tracking number via email so you can monitor your package's journey.

For orders within the continental United States, you can expect delivery within 3-5 days. International orders typically arrive within 7-10 days depending on your location. All shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location.
